Autonomous Line-Following Robot

Robotics · Sensors · Control · Arduino

Robotics

Overview

Developed an autonomous robot capable of following a line and interacting with its environment. The project combined sensor integration, motor control and structured debugging.

What I worked on

  • Integrated multiple sensors such as ToF sensors, IMU and line sensors
  • Worked on robot positioning and turn control
  • Implemented logic for movement and feedback-based correction
  • Tested the system iteratively and improved reliability

What I learned

I learned how difficult real-world sensor fusion can be compared to theory. Small errors, drift and noisy data can strongly affect the behavior of a robot, so structured testing and step-by-step debugging became essential.

Arduino C++ PID Control IMU ToF Sensors Debugging

What I worked on
